NAME

hp-testpage - Testpage Print Utility

DESCRIPTION

Print a tespage to a printer. Prints a summary of device information and shows the printer's margins.

SYNOPSIS

hp-testpage [DEVICE_URI|PRINTER_NAME] [MODE] [OPTIONS]

PRINTER|DEVICE-URI

  • -d<device-uri> or --device=<device-uri>

  • -p<printer> or --printer=<printer>

MODE

  • -i or --interactive

OPTIONS

  • -l<level> or --logging=<level> <level>: none, info*, error, warn, debug (*default)

  • -g (same as option: -ldebug)

  • -h or --help

AUTHOR
